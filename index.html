About Our Church

We are a vibrant community of believers committed to growing in faith and serving others

Our Mission

To bring people back to Christ and save lost soul by bring hope from God to the hopeless.

Our Vision

To be a church that transforms lives and communities through the love and grace of Jesus Christ.

Our Values

  • Faith: We believe in the power of God's word
  • Community: We are stronger together
  • Service: We serve others as Christ served us
  • Growth: We continually grow in our faith journey
